Section 1

Question 1:Why is one of the symbols for exclusive disjunction

'Xor' also known as exclusive disjunction means that the output is only true when exactly one of the inputs is true, not the same.

Section 2

Question 1: Why is the formula for determining the number of rows in a truth table related only to the number of inputs, and why is the formula 2^n?

Each input can only takr on two values, 0 or 1. The combination of inputs make a tree with the 1's and 0's that grows with each new input. With 1 input you have 2^1 combinations and with 2 inputs you have 2^2=4 combinations, etc.
